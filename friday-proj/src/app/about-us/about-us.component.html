About Ndlelehle Youth Advice

Ndlelehle Youth Advice

Welcome to Ndlelehle Youth Club, Ndlelehle is listing South African non-profit oragination and trying to increase awareness to the needs of the youth in this country.

This website is dedicated to the youth who wants to make difference in their lives and in the lives of others, we Ndlelehle we strongly believe that beyond youth, there is light!!!!..

Our Vision

Ndlelehle Youth Advice and Information Centre.The organisation's vision is to be effective and efficient with our center, we work with and for young people.

Currently our programmes are life skills, carreer guidance, youth camp, business skills, workshop and seminars on different topics. We try to cover every coner related to youth development; we are actually a youth development institution.

Mission

The knowledge to action foundation is a Ndlelehle commited to positively improving the quality of life of youth around the world. focusing on education, life skills and youth leadership initiatives, the Ndlelehle strive to equip young people with the essential emotion and mental tools for success.

The Ndlelehle's mission is to provide young people, regardless of background, with the strategies and confidence so that they can reach their full potential and realise their ultimate destiny.

We offer the following services:

  • HIV/AIDS & Crime awareness programme
  • Education & career path programme
  • Entrepreneurial & Life Skills programme
  • Job preparastion programme
  • Art, sport & Cultural programme
  • Computer skills programme
  • Community outreach
